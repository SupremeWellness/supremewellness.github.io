About Knowledge Said

Knowledge Said is a Master Herbalist and Certified Iridologist with deep roots in African Holistic Science and ancestral healing traditions. He studied Iridology under the late, great Dr. Paul Goss and mastered African Herbal and Spiritual Sciences through teachings from Dr. Kofi Asare of Ghana, West Africa.

Knowledge’s herbal journey is not only academic — it’s personal and cultural. Growing up in Lafayette and Opelousas, Louisiana, he was surrounded by a vibrant community of herbal wisdom. His father, a plant and soil scientist, agronomist, and self-taught herbalist, instilled in him a love for nature and the healing power of plants.

He continues to apply his knowledge and legacy through Supreme Wellness, helping others cleanse, restore, and elevate their lives through natural means rooted in ancient truths.
